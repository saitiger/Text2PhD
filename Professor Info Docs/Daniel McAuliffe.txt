# biography

Dan is a project manager for a leading Southern California general contractor, where he has successfully helped his teams build aviation, hospitality, mixed-use multi-family housing, and institutional projects throughout Los Angeles. Dan is a past recipient of the Distinguished Instructor Award at UCLA Extension for the positive impacts he made to the construction management certificate program. A licensed professional civil engineer, Dan completed his MS Civil Engineering - Construction Engineering and Management from USC and his BS Structural Engineering from UC San Diego.
