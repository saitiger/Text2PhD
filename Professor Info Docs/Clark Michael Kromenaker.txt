# biography

Clark Kromenaker is an Adjunct Lecturer in the Information Technology Program, where he teaches video game programming.

In addition to his work at USC, Clark works in the video game industry as a Lead Programmer at Hidden Variable Studios. His credited games include Bag It!, Tic Tactics, Threes, and Skullgirls Mobile.

Clark has a BS in Computer Science (Games) from USC.
