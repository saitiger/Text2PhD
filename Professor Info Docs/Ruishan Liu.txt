# biography

I am an incoming Assistant Professor of Computer Science at USC, starting January 2024. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University in 2022, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. James Zou. Currently, I am a postdoctoral scholar in the Department of Biomedical Data Science at Stanford University.
