# biography

I am a faculty member (assistant professor of research) at the Department of Computer Science and a research scientist at the Information Sciences Institute in University of Southern California. My research interests are centered around the theory and practice of distributed computing. I primarily work on algorithms and lower bounds for fault-tolerant distributed systems.
